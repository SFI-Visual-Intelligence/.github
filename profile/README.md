[Visual Intelligence](https://www.visual-intelligence.no/) aims to be the lead provider of novel deep learning-based solutions for cutting-edge complex image analysis. We answer innovation needs shared across a consortium of corporate and public user partners and beyond. Our research spans core AI methodology and domain-driven visual intelligence in medicine and health, marine science, energy, and earth observation.

## Overview 🧭

| Research challenge | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| [📉 Limited training data](#limited-training-data) | Learning from scarce, weak, partial, imbalanced, or otherwise constrained supervision. |
| [🧩 Context and dependencies](#context-and-dependencies) | Modelling structure across space, time, views, modalities, objects, and tasks. |
| [📊 Confidence and uncertainty](#confidence-and-uncertainty) | Estimating uncertainty, information, calibration, and confidence in visual intelligence systems. |
| [🔍 Explainability and reliability](#explainability-and-reliability) | Building models and evaluation methods that are interpretable, robust, trustworthy, and reliable. |

| Innovation area | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| [🩺 Medicine and health](#medicine-and-health) | Digital pathology, cardiac imaging, mammography, CT/X-ray, PET, and clinical visual decision support. |
| [🌊 Marine science](#marine-science) | Echosounder data, underwater exploration, and marine monitoring. |
| [⚡ Energy](#energy) | Power line inspection, microfossil analysis, and uncertainty-aware energy applications. |
| [🛰️ Earth observation](#earth-observation) | SAR, multimodal remote sensing, land cover mapping, sea ice, and change detection. |


## Collaborative Work 🤝

### IceNet 🧊

[Visual Intelligence](https://www.visual-intelligence.no/) is in ongoing collaboration with [The Alan Turing Institude](https://www.turing.ac.uk/), the [British Antarctic Survey](https://www.bas.ac.uk/), and the [Univerity of Cambridge](https://www.cam.ac.uk/) on AI powered sea ice forcasting. 
Detailed information on the project as well as links to corresponding GitHub repositories can be found on the [IceNet website](https://icenet.ai/).
